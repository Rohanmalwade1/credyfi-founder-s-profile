Problem-first, not product-first

The biggest barrier for first-time investors isn’t pricing or features — it’s trust. People want a real person who explains things clearly, doesn’t push them, and stays responsible over time. CredyFi is built for that moment when someone wants to start, but needs reassurance more than a sales pitch.
